Cache Monet

I really enjoy this site. The random images being generated as you clicks really catches my attention. The song plays in a loop giving this website and endless feel. I enjoy digital art very much, and this website really captures everything i enjoy about digital art, like the weird feeling you get at the collage or images.



Vector Park

This website is simple, but very entertaining. Every click engages the user to find something new. You pull off the nose to reveal a different nose each time. The eye will cry if you click it, and then comes rolling out the mouth. This website is very strange. However i find it very fun and interactive.
